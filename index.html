Our Introduction

Who we are

PLC Group in a bid to offer value added service now offers SafePass, a platform that enables personnel to get their OSP online at their fingertips. SafePass ensures a secure and hassle-free process whenever personnel within your organization are required to obtain their OSP to go offshore.

Oil Industry

SafePass is an online tool used by offshore oil and gas operators and service providers that holds up to date information on offshore personnel trainning ,records,medical certificates. and details of other important competencies. This provides an electronic safety filter to ensure everyone travelling offshore has successfully completed the proper training.Additionally SafePass also holds details of persons to contact in case of an emergency.
